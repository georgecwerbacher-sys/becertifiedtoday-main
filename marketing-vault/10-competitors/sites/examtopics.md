# ExamTopics — SY0-701 (adjacent competitor)

## Model

- **Free** web access to community-contributed **“actual exam”** Q&A (**611+** visible for SY0-701)  
- Paid **Pro** unlock (discussions, full set)  
- **Not PDF-first** — competes for same **search intent** as dump sites  
- **Monthly poll:** page 1 of `/view/1/` (10 questions) for discovery vs BCT — community votes **not** verification

## Positioning

Anti–price-gouge story; still uses **braindump** language historically. Clone sites steal their content per their own marketing.

## Policy

CompTIA lists **brain dumps** and **actual exam questions** as unauthorized — same bucket as PDF dumps.

## Weakness vs BCT

- No **timed 90-min** exam simulation  
- No **interactive PBQ** (drag-and-drop IR, multi-step)  
- Answer quality = **community vote**, not verified explanations  
- Encourages memorization of recalled items  

## BCT angle

- “**Free sample** proves UI + explanation quality” without policy-gray “actual questions”  
- Portal **adaptive** + **domain** tracking
