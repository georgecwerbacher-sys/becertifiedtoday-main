# PDF dump market — competitive analysis (SY0-701)

Deep dive on sites that sell **printable PDF / braindump question banks** for CompTIA Security+ **SY0-701** — **not** compared to video courses, bootcamps, or official CertMaster.

**Tracker:** `competitors-pdf-dumps-tracker.csv` (import into Numbers per [[how-to-use-tracker-in-numbers]]).

---

## Executive summary

The “dump” market for Security+ is a **fragmented SEO arbitrage layer**: dozens of brands, a handful of **syndicated product templates** (~891 questions + 1,003-page PDF study guide), and delivery that is **PDF-first** or **VCE/ETE desktop files**. Real **browser-based PBQ simulations** (drag-and-drop, multi-step IR scenarios) are rare; when “simulation” appears in marketing, it usually means **a few hotspot items inside a desktop test engine**, not exam-faithful performance labs.

**Be Certified Today (BCT)** competes on the **same buyer intent** (“pass Security+ fast”) but should **never mirror dump language** in ads ([[../01-strategy/positioning-and-messaging|positioning]]). Differentiators that matter to buyers burned by dumps:

| Dump market norm | BCT counter |
|------------------|-------------|
| Static PDF / memorization | **700+** interactive questions in browser |
| Desktop VCE/ETE install | **No third-party player**; phone/tablet/desktop |
| Stale SY0-601-era items | **SY0-701 objectives v5.0** alignment |
| “Actual exam questions” | **Verified explanations**; exam prep only |
| Weak or fake PBQ | **Hot-spot sims** + **90-minute timed exam** |
| One-size cram | **Adaptive review** + domain progress |

CompTIA classifies these materials as **unauthorized** and may revoke certifications ([unauthorized training FAQ](https://www.comptia.org/en/resources/test-policies/unauthorized-training-materials-faq/)).

## Market structure

### 1. PDF + test engine retailers

Single-exam storefronts: **DumpsMate**, **DumpsPDF**, **Exams4Sure**, **CertBolt**, **Pass4Sure**, **ITPass4Sure**, **Certs4Sure**.

**Model:** Add to cart → instant download → PDF printable Q&A; upsell “testing engine” or “desktop simulator.”

**Typical price band (SY0-701):** ~$45–$125 for Q&A; +$50 for PDF addon; study guide PDF ~$25–$40.

**Copy patterns:** “real exam questions,” “verified by experts,” “100% pass guarantee,” “first attempt,” “updated May/June 2026.”

### 2. Syndicated marketplace cluster (PrepAway ecosystem)

**PrepAway**, **ExamCollection**, **Exam-Labs** share:

- **891** premium questions & answers  
- **1,003-page** PDF study guide  
- **167-lecture** training course (video — still dump-adjacent bundle, not our course comp)  
- **VCE/ETE** files requiring **Vumingo / Avanset** desktop players  
- “Last week results” social proof (e.g. 11,660 passes, 92.9% “word for word”)

**Implication:** Competitors are often **the same inventory** with different domains — not independent 700+ item banks.

### 3. Subscription dump clubs

**ActualTests** — ~**$149** unlimited access to thousands of exam PDFs/engines across vendors.

**Buyer:** certification collectors; **not** the same as BCT’s $19.99 / 30-day single-exam focus.

### 4. Community braindump (adjacent)

**ExamTopics** — free/paid **web** Q&A (611+ visible threads), heavy “actual exam” framing, **no** legitimate PBQ lab.

**Policy risk** for users; **SEO risk** for us if we target “actual questions” keywords.

### 5. White-label / clone storefronts

**Pass4SureHub**, lower-price clones of Pass4Sure/Exams4Sure templates.

## UX & monetization patterns (why buyers bounce)

### Popup / interruption stack (typical **High → Very High**)

Observed category behaviors (verify per site over time):

- Exit-intent and **discount countdown** banners  
- **Live chat** (“purchase help”) on every page  
- **Free dump** teaser → immediate upsell (“premium file or fail”)  
- **ETE download** interstitial pushing Vumingo install  
- Fake **“last week pass rate”** tickers  

BCT should stay **clean**: sticky CTA + portal gate only — reinforces trust vs dump malls.

### Outdated content signals

Watch for these on competitor pages (common):

- Still selling **SY0-601** bundles or “601 may overlap” filler  
- **Identical 891 Q** count across unrelated brands (clone, not curation)  
- **1003-page** identical study guide (syndicated PDF)  
- **“Real exam questions”** without objective version (v5.0)  
- **Word-for-word** pass claims (misleading; policy violation)  
- DumpsPDF-style stale **footer exam year** typos  

BCT messaging: **“current objectives v5.0”**, **700+** owned bank, **recent-style** wording.

### Simulation gap

Dump sites list **hotspot: 2**, **simulation: 3** in SKU breakdown (DumpsMate example) while advertising “full exam experience.” SY0-701 requires **performance-based** competency across scenarios — PDF cannot deliver **dark web IR**, **drag-and-drop architecture**, or **multi-pane** UI.

**Sales line:** “If your prep is a PDF, you have never practiced a PBQ under time pressure.”
