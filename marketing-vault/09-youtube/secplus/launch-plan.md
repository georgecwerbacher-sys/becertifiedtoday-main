## Channel positioning (say this in every series)

| Say | Do not say |
|-----|------------|
| SY0-701 **exam prep** and **practice** | “Full Security+ course” / “40-hour training” |
| **Timed simulation** in the browser | “Guaranteed pass” |
| **Try free sample** on the site | “Actual exam questions” / dumps |
| PBQ-style **practice** with explanations | Implied CompTIA endorsement |
| Works on **phone, tablet, desktop** | “Only on desktop” (unless demoing a known limitation) |

**Differentiator vs YouTube course creators:** you show **doing the exam-like item** in the real product (or faithful screen capture), not slides for hours.

## Content pillars (Security+ phase 1)

| Pillar | Length | Goal | CTA |
|--------|--------|------|-----|
| **A — Product commercial** | 45–90 sec | Trust + “how it works” | Free 35-min sim |

## Pillar A — Commercial / product videos (script beats)

Record **screen + voice** on real pages (staging OK; label if not prod). Show cursor, timer, and scorecard where relevant.

### A1 — “Security+ exam prep in your browser” (flagship, ~75 sec)

1. Hook (5s): “No PDF. No install. SY0-701 practice that feels like test day.”
2. Free 35-min sample — email unlock, timer, Back / mark for review (15s).
3. Domain scorecard at end (10s).
4. Paid 90-min sim — $9.99, one attempt, mix of MCQ + PBQ (15s).
5. Portal mention — 10/30-day access, question bank (10s).
6. CTA (10s): link to free sim + on-screen URL.

**Capture:** `comptia-sec+-home.html#secplus-lead-capture` → run sim → scorecard blur if PII.

### A2 — “Adaptive practice mode” (~60 sec)

**Be accurate:** Practice hub supports adaptive session options ([[../06-website-optimization/pages/comptia-sec-plus-home|home]] / portal). If UI shows “coming soon” on a control, say **“adaptive extra questions in practice mode”** only where enabled — do not overclaim on Training Portal radios that are disabled.

1. Show starting a practice set with adaptive option (if live).
2. Explain: extra items weighted toward weak domains / missed objectives (plain language).
3. Contrast: **timed 90-min sim = fixed full exam-style run**, not adaptive.

### A3 — “Phone and tablet” (~45 sec)

1. Same site on phone: sample question or short practice item.
2. Touch: drag-and-drop or tap targets (use a PBQ or D&D sample if possible).
3. Voice: “Use Safari/Chrome — no app store install.”
4. CTA: free sim link (works on mobile).

### A4 — “Realistic SY0-701 timed exam” (~90 sec)

1. Show purchase card copy: 90 min, ~90 questions, PBQ types.
2. Show timer, question navigation, mark for review.
3. End: domain breakdown — not a pass/fail guarantee.
4. CTA: try free 35-min first.

### A5 — “OpenSSL / PBQ simulation” (~60 sec) — optional week 3

Drive to: `/COMP_TIA_SEC+/SEC+_Sim_Hot_Spot/simulation-secure-web-architecture-openssl.html`  
Supports [[../07-keywords/landing-maps/security-plus-openssl-pbq|OpenSSL PBQ]] keywords later.

| B5 | “How to use mark for review” in timed sim | Free or paid sim UI | 2 min |

**Title pattern:** `SY0-701 Practice: [topic] — Exam-Style Question (+ Explanation)`  
**Description:** first line = free practice link with UTM ([[site-integration-and-utm|UTM doc]]).

**Policy:** Teach the **objective**, not “what was on last week’s exam.”
