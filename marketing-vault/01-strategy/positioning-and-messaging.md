# Positioning & messaging — Be Certified Today

North star for Google Ads, landing pages, AI search visibility, and all site copy.

## What we are

**Exam preparation** — not a course, not instructor-led training, not a certification program.

We help people who are **already studying for a specific exam** practice with:

- **Latest question styles** aligned to current exam objectives (e.g. SY0-701)
- **Verified solutions** and explanations — not unvetted dumps
- **Exam-realistic formats**: multiple-choice, performance-based / drag-and-drop, timed full simulations
- **Everything in the browser** — no PDF downloads, no third-party apps, no separate lab software

## What we are not

| Avoid in ads & landing copy | Why |
|----------------------------|-----|
| “Course”, “training program”, “learn from scratch” | Wrong buyer — we serve test-takers, not beginners shopping for education |
| “Guaranteed pass” | Policy risk; inaccurate promise |
| “Brain dump”, “actual exam questions”, “leaked” | Policy + trust; we sell **prep**, not cheating |
| Implied CompTIA / vendor endorsement | Unless formally licensed |

Internal URLs may say “Training Portal” — **customer-facing copy** should prefer **practice portal**, **exam prep**, **simulation**, **question bank**.

## Primary customer

Someone who:

- Has a **test date in mind** (or failed once and wants to pass next time)
- Wants practice that feels **as close to the real exam as possible** — timing, question mix, interaction types
- Wants **confidence** before sitting the exam
- Wants to **avoid retake fees** ($$$) by preparing properly the first time
- Is tired of **PDFs**, static files, and **third-party software** that doesn’t match exam day

**Emotional job:** “I need to know I’m ready.”  
**Functional job:** “Give me realistic practice with trustworthy answers in one place.”

### Federal & defense segment (Security+ SY0-701)

Many DoD, federal civilian, and defense-contractor cyber roles use **CompTIA Security+** toward baseline qualification under the **DoD Cyberspace Workforce Framework (DoD 8140)**—requirements vary by work role and contract. This is a high-intent segment with **deadline pressure** (new hire windows, role changes, re-certification).

→ Full playbook: [[security-plus-federal-defense-foundation|Security+ federal & defense foundation]] (people, times, locations, keywords, geo ads).

**Cisco (CCNA / ENCOR):** Same exam-prep rules; active Google Ads on `ccna-home.html` and `ccnp-home.html`. See [[cisco-certifications-exam-prep-foundation|Cisco certifications foundation]].

**Say:** exam prep for SY0-701; browser practice for shift workers and TDY; timed simulation before test day.  
**Do not say:** we satisfy your DoD requirement; guaranteed qualification; clearance support.

## Core value pillars (use everywhere)

1. **Exam-realistic** — timed sims, PBQ-style items, domain-organized banks  
2. **Verified & current** — solutions checked against official objectives / sources (see repo answer-verification rules for content work)  
3. **Browser-only** — open and practice; no installs  
4. **Pay for access, not a subscription** — 10-day / 30-day passes; one-time sim purchase  
5. **Confidence economics** — cheaper than a retake; practice until ready  

**Promotions:** Default is **list price + free sample**, not coupon-led offers during paid launch. See [[promotions-and-coupons|promotions & coupons]].

## Proof points to surface on landing pages

- Free sample before purchase
- SY0-701 objective alignment (Security+); equivalent for CCNA / ENCOR
- Interactive items (not flat PDF)
- Timed simulation length / format called out explicitly
- Clear pricing before checkout

## Voice & tone

- Direct, practical, respectful of the exam-taker’s time  
- Confident about **practice quality**, humble about **outcomes** (prep, not promise)  
- Specific exam codes and formats (SY0-701, PBQ, 90-minute sim) beat generic “cybersecurity learning”
