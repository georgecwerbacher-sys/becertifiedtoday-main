# AI Amplified Marketer — working notes

Personal notes while reading. Use AI for speed and synthesis; keep human judgment on strategy, creative, and what to test next.

## Core ideas (fill in as you read)

- AI amplifies the marketer — it does not replace intuition or creative choices.
- Data informs decisions; it is not the whole story.
- PPC / paid search remains a primary lever; automation changes *how* you operate, not *whether* measurement matters.
