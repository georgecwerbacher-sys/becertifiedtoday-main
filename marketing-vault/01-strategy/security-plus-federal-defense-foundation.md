# Security+ federal & defense foundation

Security+ SY0-701 is a **compliance and workforce qualification exam** for many U.S. federal, DoD, and defense-contractor cyber roles—not only a generic IT cert. Be Certified Today sells **exam prep** (practice + simulation), not clearance processing, HR policy, or official DoD certification tracking.

Related: [[positioning-and-messaging]] · [[../02-campaigns/security-plus/security-plus-google-ads|Security+ campaign]] · [[../06-website-optimization/pages/comptia-sec-plus-home|landing page]] · [[cisco-certifications-exam-prep-foundation|Cisco CCNA & ENCOR foundation]] (shared contractor/geo patterns) · [[../08-8140-compliance/marketing-incorporation-plan|8140 incorporation plan]] (official PDF review)

## Why this segment matters

| Driver                              | Implication for messaging                                                                                                                                |
| ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **DoD Cyberspace Workforce (8140)** | Many military/civilian/contractor cyber roles map to baseline certifications; Security+ is widely used toward those baselines for applicable work roles. |
| **Contract clauses**                | Prime and sub contracts often require named certifications within a window after hire or role change.                                                    |
| **Federal hiring**                  | Agencies hire cyber staff on rolling and fiscal-year cycles; new hires face internal deadlines to qualify.                                               |
| **Retake cost + schedule pressure** | Missing an internal deadline can block system access, billable role, or promotion—prep quality matters.                                                  |
| **Browser-only prep**               | TDY, shift work, secure facilities, and PCS moves favor study that does not require lab VMs or installs.                                                 |

**Compliance disclaimer (use on site and in ads):** Certification requirements depend on your **work role, component, and contract**. Under DoDM 8140.03, DoD personnel generally must achieve **foundational** qualification within **9 months** and **residential** qualification within **12 months** of assignment to a cyberspace work role—**your component may set shorter deadlines or additional requirements**. Confirm the exact cert and deadline with your **DoD Cyber Workforce Program representative**, security manager, or contracting officer. Be Certified Today does not grant clearance or DoD qualification—only helps you prepare for vendor exams (e.g., CompTIA SY0-701).

Official reference: [[../08-8140-compliance/marketing-incorporation-plan|8140 incorporation plan]] · [DoD qualification matrices](https://public.cyber.mil/wid/dod8140/dod-cyber-workforce-qualifications-matrices-management/)

## People — who we speak to

### Primary personas

| Persona | Situation | Message hook |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **DoD civilian / military cyber** | Assigned IAT/IAM or work-role baseline; internal deadline | “Practice SY0-701 before your qualification window closes.” |
| **Defense contractor** | New hire or new SOW; cert named in offer or DD254 flow | “Contract-ready exam prep—browser practice between shifts.” |
| **Federal agency IT/cyber** | DHS, VA, Treasury, GSA, etc.; role-based cert plans | “Federal workforce exam prep—not a bootcamp course.” |
| **Career transitioner** | Leaving military or commercial IT; targeting cleared contract work | “Build Security+ readiness before you apply.” |
| **Re-certification** | Security+ expires on CompTIA’s three-year cycle; CEU or re-exam | “Refresh with timed sim + domain banks before you re-test.” |

### Roles often in the Security+ conversation (not exhaustive)

- Cyber defense analyst, security administrator, systems administrator (cyber track)
- Help desk / tier-2 moving into security operations
- ISSO support, vulnerability management, SOC tier-1
- Network defender on contract programs requiring baseline certs

### Who we are **not** targeting with federal-specific ads

- Clearance investigators or clearance processing searches
- People shopping for “Security+ certificate without exam”
- Pure policy/compliance officers who never sit SY0-701

## Times — when demand spikes

Use for **ad scheduling**, **email capture timing**, and **landing copy urgency** (without fake countdowns).

| Timing trigger | Typical window | Marketing angle |
|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **DoD 8140 foundational deadline** | **9 months** from assignment (DoD baseline; components may be stricter) | “New cyberspace work role—prep before your qualification window.” |
| **New hire qualification** | Often aligned to 9-month foundational window; verify internally | “Started a cyber role with a cert deadline?” |
| **Role / billet change** | 30–90 days before access revocation | “New work role—qualify before losing system access.” |
| **Contract start / option year** | Contract kickoff + first 60 days | “New contract team—batch prep for SY0-701.” |
| **PCS / TDY** | 30–60 days before move | “Study in the browser from lodging or new duty station.” |
| **Fiscal year hiring** | Aug–Oct (federal); rolling for contractors | “Fall hiring surge—prep before test-center slots fill.” |
| **Pre-exam cram** | 2–6 weeks before scheduled SY0-701 | “30-day pass + 90-minute timed sim.” |
| **Re-certification** | 3–6 months before CompTIA expiration | “SY0-701 refresh—don’t let baseline lapse.” |
| **Night / weekend study** | Evenings, weekends, drill weekends | “No installs—open practice on your schedule.” |

## Locations — where to find them

### Geo targeting (Google Ads — metros & corridors)

Start with **location bid adjustments** or dedicated ad groups; radius 25–50 mi around core employment centers.

| Tier | Markets | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Tier 1** | Washington DC; Arlington/Alexandria/Fairfax VA; Bethesda/Silver Spring MD; Colorado Springs CO; San Antonio TX; Norfolk VA | High density of DoD HQ, agencies, and cyber contract labor |
| **Tier 2** | Huntsville AL; Tampa FL; San Diego CA; Columbia/Fort Meade MD corridor; Wright-Patterson (Dayton) OH; Augusta GA (Fort Eisenhower) | Major installations and contractor campuses |
| **Tier 3** | Oklahoma City OK; Omaha NE (Offutt); St. Louis MO (Scott); Honolulu HI; El Paso TX; Fayetteville NC | Secondary bases + growing cyber contract work |

**Nationwide remote:** Keep a national campaign for “security+ practice test” / “sy0-701 exam prep”; use federal/defense ad copy variants in Tier 1–2 only to measure lift.

### On-page “where you study” (product fit)

- **CONUS duty stations** — browser prep from government laptop or personal device (follow local policy)
- **OCONUS / TDY** — no VM install; timed sim in browser
- **Contractor site / SCIF-adjacent** — practice questions where installs are blocked
- **PCS in progress** — same portal on new device after checkout email restore flow

Do **not** claim suitability for classified systems—users follow local IA policy.

## Message pillars (federal-specific)

1. **Compliance context, prep product** — “Meet your work-role baseline with SY0-701 exam prep.”
2. **Deadline-aware** — internal windows, contract starts, re-cert (no guaranteed pass)
3. **Browser-only** — study from duty station, hotel, or home without lab software
4. **Exam-realistic** — PBQ + timed sim mirrors test-day pressure
5. **Economics** — cheaper than retake + missed contract billability
