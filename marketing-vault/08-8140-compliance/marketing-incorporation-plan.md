## Program summary (for marketers)

### 8570 → 8140

DoD **8570** IA certification tiers (Levels 1–3 by computing/network/enclave environment) transitioned to **8140**, which qualifies personnel by **DCWF work role** and **proficiency level**, not only IA hierarchy. *(Source: program intro, p.2)*

### Seven Cyber Workforce Elements

Information Technology (IT) · Cybersecurity (CS) · Cyber Effects (CE) · Intelligence (Cyber) · Data/AI · Software Engineering (SE) · Cyber Enablers *(program intro, p.1)*

**Be Certified Today product fit (element-level, not cert-specific yet):**

| Element | Likely products | Confirm via matrix |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **CS** | Security+ SY0-701 | Which work roles list Security+ / CompTIA |
| **IT** | CCNA 200-301, CCNP ENCOR 350-401 | Network/system admin work roles |
| CE, Intel, Data/AI, SE, Enablers | Future courses | TBD |

### Qualification model (DoDM 8140.03)

1. **Foundational** — complete **one of**: education, training, or **personnel certification** (ISO 17024–accredited), mapped to work role + proficiency in the qualification matrix.
2. **Residential** — on-the-job / supervised qualification (12 months).
3. **CPD** — 20 hours/year after foundational + residential complete.

**Components may require more than one foundational option** (e.g., cert + training). *(DoDM 8140.03 §3.2.a.(5))*

**Contractors:** foundational qualification at **commencement** of cyberspace work; residential only if contract requires. *(DoDM 8140.03 §4.2.b)*

### Official timelines (use in copy)

| Requirement | DoD baseline | Source |
|-------------|--------------|--------|
| Foundational qualification | **9 months** from assignment to cyberspace work role | DoDM 8140.03 §4.2.a.(2)(a); Matrix SOP p.6 |
| Residential qualification | **12 months** from assignment | DoDM 8140.03 §4.2.a.(2)(b); Matrix SOP p.6 |

Replace vague “90–180 days” in marketing with **“9 months (DoD baseline—your component may set shorter deadlines)”** plus **verify with your Cyber Workforce Program representative**.

### Where certs are listed

Commercial certifications (Security+, CCNA, etc.) appear in the **Qualification Matrix** on DoD Cyber Exchange—not in DoDM 8140.03 text. Matrix SOP: public URL above; refreshed **quarterly** for CS and IT elements.
