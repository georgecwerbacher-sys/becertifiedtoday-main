Timed CCNA exam

CCNA Practice Test Simulation

You have 120 minutes to complete the CCNA exam. Questions are drawn randomly from a bank of 500, so each attempt is unique and demands solid command of the material.

The exam includes multiple-choice questions, drag-and-drop items, and at least three labs, with only Next and Quit available for navigation. Once you advance past a question, you cannot return to it, and if you exit the exam, the attempt ends immediately.

Because each attempt costs $300, it's worth approaching the exam with a disciplined strategy, careful pacing, and full confidence in your preparation. At the conclusion, a score report is generated showing pass or fail along with your performance details.

Content on this site is for training and study practice only. There is no guarantee that any question, lab, or scenario here will appear on a live certification exam, or that using this site alone will prepare you to pass.
