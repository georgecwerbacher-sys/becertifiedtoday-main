CCNA Timed Test Simulation

Welcome to the CCNA simulated exam. The exam timer is set for 120 minutes, the same total time as the real CCNA certification exam. No downloads and no third-party apps are required—everything runs in your browser on BeCertifiedToday.com.

What’s included

  • Random multiple-choice items from the CCNA question bank of over 500 questions
  • Tests are adaptive, and each test is randomly generated
  • Random drag-and-drop exercises
  • Random CLI lab simulations
  • One continuous 120-minute countdown for the full session (single clock)

Simulation Test Options

  • Next only — you cannot return to an earlier item. A page refresh or trying to go back to a previous page in the browser will exit the simulated exam.
  • Reset, Next, and Quit in the exam footer
  • CLI labs only: Spoiler shows the exact commands—use it only if you need them. CLI labs are designed with helper shortcuts and unneeded commands disabled. Multiple-choice and drag-and-drop have no Spoiler.
  • When the last item is answered or time runs out: a results page shows overall score and performance by exam objective. Quit sends you straight back to the CCNA study hub.
  • After Continue on results: if you have active CCNA portal access, you return to the CCNA Training Portal; otherwise one-time test access ends and you can purchase again from this page when ready.
