CCNA timed test simulation

This is the landing page for the timed simulation: use Purchase timed test access if you do not have paid access yet, or Start timed test when you already have access (for example after completing Stripe Checkout or using a saved entry link). One purchase unlocks one timed attempt in a single 120-minute window with one clock.

What’s included

  • Random multiple-choice items from the CCNA question bank
  • Random drag-and-drop exercises
  • Random CLI lab simulations
  • One continuous 120-minute countdown for the full session (single clock)

Runner options

  • Next only — you cannot return to an earlier item
  • Reset, Next, and Quit in the exam footer
  • Command line: type spoiler and press Enter for a short hint (no Helper UI)
  • When the last item is answered, time runs out, or you quit: a results page shows overall score and performance by exam objective
  • After Continue on results: if you have active 30-day portal access, you return to the CCNA Training Portal; otherwise one-time test access ends and you return here to purchase again when ready
