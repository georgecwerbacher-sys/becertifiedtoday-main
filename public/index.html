FAQ

Access & devices

How sign-in links work, switching phones or browsers, and using the site on mobile.

  • Do I need a membership?

    No. Be Certified Today does not sell a recurring membership. You choose access for a defined period (for example, 30 days of full library access on this site) and only pay again if you want another period. Sample questions and labs on this homepage stay available without purchase.

  • Do I need additional software like GNS3 or IOU to run the labs?

    No. No downloads are required to practice the labs—it is all built into the testing engine.

  • My link failed on a new device or browser—what should I do?

    Access links are validated for the device and browser where you open them. If you switch phones, use another laptop, change browsers, or open a private/incognito window, treat it like a new environment: go to the ENCOR portal and request a new access email using the same address you used at checkout, and complete the steps on that device and browser.

    Full ENCOR training: becertifiedtoday-encor.vercel.app

  • What does a 90-day access link cover?

    When your flow includes a 90-day device or admin-style link, training use on that paired setup is unlimited for the whole period. You are not limited to a fixed number of logins during those 90 days—just normal, reasonable personal study use on that device.

  • Can I study on a phone or iPad?

    Yes. Practice runs in the browser on desktops, phones, and tablets (including iPad). Use an up-to-date Safari, Chrome, or Firefox. Drag-and-drop and lab layouts are built to work with touch and pointer input.

    If a page misbehaves on mobile, request a fresh access link from that same phone or tablet so your session matches where you actually train.

  • Will anyone call or email me for payment information?

    No. Be Certified Today does not collect customer details for resale or marketing, and nobody from the site will contact you to ask for payment card numbers or personal information. Complete purchases only through the official checkout flow; use official magic-link or portal pages for access.
