BeCertifiedToday Hybrid PKI Audit — Part 1

BeCertifiedToday runs a hybrid PKI: offline root on-premises, two intermediate CAs (on-prem and Azure), and leaf certificates for TLS and code signing. An audit flagged a deprecated intermediate signing algorithm, an expired leaf, and a CRL that is 11 days old (maximum allowed: 7 days).
