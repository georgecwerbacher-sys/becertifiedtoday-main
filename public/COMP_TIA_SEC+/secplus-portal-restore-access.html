Restore Security+ training portal access

Purchases unlock this site by saving an access window in your browser. Signing out of Stripe does not remove that by itself—but clearing cookies/site data, using private browsing, or switching devices means you need to activate again from Stripe proof you paid.

  • Paste your Stripe Checkout Session ID below if you have it (starts with cs_live_… or cs_test_…). You can copy it from the Stripe Dashboard under Payments for your charge, or from a bookmarked success URL that still includes ?session_id=cs_….
  • Stripe receipt email confirms payment but usually does not log you into this site—there is no password yet. Bookmark your post-checkout page or keep this session ID somewhere safe if you routinely clear browser storage.
  • Email link: If the site sent you a magic link at checkout (webhook + Resend configured), you can request another from Email me a portal link while your purchase is still active—use the same email you typed at Stripe.
Enter your checkout session ID and verify. Access only restores if payment completed and your Stripe access window has not ended.
