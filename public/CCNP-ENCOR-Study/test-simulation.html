ENCOR Timed Test Simulation

Welcome to the ENCOR simulated exam. The exam timer is set for 120 minutes. No downloads and no third-party apps are required—everything runs in your browser from the ENCOR question bank, drag-and-drop set, and CLI labs.

What’s included

  • 50 random multiple-choice items
  • 5 random drag-and-drop exercises
  • 4 random CLI lab simulations
  • Mixed order each run — all 59 items shuffled under one clock
  • One continuous 120-minute countdown for the full session (single clock)

Simulation Test Options

  • Next only — you cannot return to an earlier item. A page refresh or trying to go back to a previous page in the browser will exit the simulated exam.
  • Next and Quit in the exam footer
  • CLI labs only: helper shortcuts and unneeded commands are disabled where the lab supports it. Multiple-choice and drag-and-drop use the standard practice pages.
  • When the last item is answered or time runs out: a results scorecard shows your scaled score, performance by ENCOR domain, and objectives that need the most work.
  • After the run: return to the ENCOR Training Portal to review banks, drag-and-drop, or labs.
