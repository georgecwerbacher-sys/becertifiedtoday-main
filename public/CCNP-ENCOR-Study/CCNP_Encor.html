Lab Contents

ENCOR subject areas covered

Cisco ENCOR content in this library follows the official 350-401 ENCOR v1.2 blueprint: Architecture, Virtualization, Infrastructure, Network Assurance, Security, and Automation and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Architecture (15%): enterprise design principles, high availability, Catalyst SD-WAN, SD-Access, and QoS configurations.
  • Virtualization (10%): hypervisors, virtual machines, virtual switching, VRF, GRE, IPsec tunneling, LISP, and VXLAN.
  • Infrastructure (30%): Layer 2 trunking, EtherChannel, RSTP/MST, STP enhancements, EIGRP and OSPF concepts, OSPFv2/v3 configuration, eBGP, policy-based routing, NTP/PTP, NAT/PAT, FHRP, and multicast.
  • Network Assurance (10%): troubleshooting with debugs, traceroute, ping, SNMP, and syslog; Flexible NetFlow; SPAN/RSPAN/ERSPAN; IP SLA; Cisco Catalyst Center; NETCONF; and RESTCONF.
  • Security (20%): device access control, AAA, ACLs, CoPP, REST API security, threat defense, endpoint security, next-generation firewalls, TrustSec, and MACsec.
  • Automation and Artificial Intelligence (15%): Python, JSON, YANG, Cisco Catalyst Center and SD-WAN Manager APIs, REST API responses, EEM applets, and agent versus agentless orchestration tools.

Timed sessions are written to reinforce these weighted exam domains so your study time stays aligned with ENCOR objectives.
