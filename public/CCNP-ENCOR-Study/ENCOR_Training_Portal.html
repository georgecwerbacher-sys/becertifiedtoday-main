Study path

ENCOR 350-401 blueprint and domain weights

CCNP ENCOR study lines up with six main knowledge domains. Cisco publishes the current 350-401 ENCOR v1.2 exam areas as architecture, virtualization, infrastructure, network assurance, security, and automation and artificial intelligence. Official guidance puts the strongest emphasis on Infrastructure (30%), followed by Security (20%) and Architecture (15%).

ENCOR study sections

Domain weights and section numbers below come from the Cisco 350-401 ENCOR v1.2 exam topics (PDF)—use them to budget study hours, not as a guarantee of item counts on your specific attempt. The live exam is 120 minutes with a mix of multiple-choice and simulation-style items.

What to focus on first

Start with infrastructure and architecture—they underpin routing, switching, and a large share of configuration-style prompts. Layer in security and network assurance next, then virtualization and automation for the full ENCOR mix.

Study order suggestion

  1. Architecture and design principles
  2. Infrastructure — Layer 2, routing, and path selection
  3. Security — ACLs, CoPP, AAA, and device hardening
  4. Network assurance — monitoring, telemetry, and troubleshooting tools
  5. Virtualization — VRF, overlays, and tunneling
  6. Automation and artificial intelligence — APIs, Python, JSON, and programmability

Under time pressure, leaning hardest into Infrastructure and Security before you polish lighter domains mirrors where Cisco weights the ENCOR blueprint.
