On a phone or tablet, tap a chip to pick it up, then tap a drop zone (or another chip to swap).

Drag and drop the virtual components from the left onto their descriptions on the right.

Drag each component into the box beside the description it matches. Use Check Answer when done, or Reset to clear the slots.
