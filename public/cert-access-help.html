Certification access help

Use this page for account access support across all certification training portals, including getting a fresh magic link on another device.

Request a new magic link (for another device)

Enter the same email address used during checkout. If your access is active, we will send a fresh sign-in link.

How access works

  • Checkout grants access to the certification linked to the purchase email.
  • Magic links are one-time and expire after a short window for security.
  • Each device/browser needs its own sign-in via magic link.

Troubleshooting

  • Use the newest email link only (older links expire or become invalid once used).
  • Check Spam, Promotions, and All Mail folders.
  • If your access has expired, renew the certification subscription first.

Access training area

  • After sign-in, you will be redirected to the training portal linked to your access.
  • Current CCNP ENCOR portal: becertifiedtoday-encor.vercel.app
