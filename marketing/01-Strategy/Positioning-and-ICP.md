# Positioning & ideal customer profile

## Positioning statement

For **IT professionals and students preparing for Cisco CCNA or CCNP exams**, **Be Certified Today** is a **browser-based certification lab** that delivers **practice questions, CLI labs, drag-and-drop items, and timed exam simulations**—unlike **static PDFs or questionable dump sites**, our product is **interactive, structured, and aligned with how Cisco tests skills**.

---

## Ideal customer profiles

### ICP 1 — Career switcher (primary for CCNA)

- **Situation:** 0–3 years in IT or breaking in; studying for **CCNA 200-301**
- **Pain:** Overwhelmed by resources; wants one place to practice hands-on
- **Trigger:** Exam date booked or job posting requires CCNA
- **Message:** Structured practice + simulation before exam day

### ICP 2 — Working network admin (CCNP / upskill)

- **Situation:** Already certified or experienced; pursuing **ENCOR** or role change
- **Pain:** Needs efficient review, not beginner videos
- **Message:** Deep question bank + advanced topics on `/ccnp-home.html`

### ICP 3 — Repeat test-taker

- **Situation:** Failed attempt; needs timed practice under pressure
- **Pain:** Didn’t finish exam in time or weak on simlets
- **Message:** **Timed CCNA exam simulation** + labs

---

## Jobs to be done

1. “I need to know if I’m ready for the real exam.” → timed simulation  
2. “I need to practice CLI and troubleshooting.” → CLI labs  
3. “I need more questions than my book.” → question bank / hub  

---

## Competitive frame (for ad copy, not public bashing)

| Alternative | Weakness we exploit |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Free YouTube only | No timed exam, no structured bank |
| PDF / ebook packs | Not interactive; no labs |
| Dump sites | Policy risk, no real learning |
| Bootcamps | Cost, schedule |

---

## Voice & tone

- Direct, professional, encouraging  
- Cisco-friendly (cert names correct: CCNA 200-301, CCNP ENCOR)  
- No hype guarantees
