## What I built Be Certified Today for

I built this site because I know what exam day feels like — the pressure, the second-guessing, the worry that your prep material is already out of date. I wanted a place where you can practice in peace, see honest feedback, and walk in **ready**, not rattled.

My goal is simple: **help you finish what you started and earn the certification you are working toward.**

## What I promise you (most important first)

1. **Material that stays current** — I align practice to today’s exam objectives (CCNA v1.1, ENCOR v1.2, Security+ v5.0) and keep adding questions. You are not stuck memorizing a PDF that stopped updating last year.

2. **Practice that feels like the real exam** — Timed runs, drag-and-drop, CLI labs, and mixed question types — presented the way you will see them on test day, not as a random list of trivia.

3. **Answers I stand behind** — I verify keys and explanations against **official, highly relevant sources** — Cisco documentation, CompTIA objectives, and vendor learning materials. I do not copy dump sites or treat “most upvoted” forum posts as gospel.

4. **No membership games** — You can try free samples without signing up for an account. If you purchase, you get **clear access for a set period, one time** — not a subscription that nags you every month. I do not ask for more personal information than checkout needs, and I do not sell your contact details.

5. **A quiet place to study** — When you are inside the practice portal, I keep it **free of third-party ads and pop-ups** so you can focus. (You may see a one-time offer on the homepage; the library itself is for studying.)

6. **Guidance that matches how the exam works** — In labs and online scenarios, I give you **clear, exam-style instructions** — how to read the task, use the interface, and work through multi-step items the way the certification experience expects.

7. **Confidence when it counts** — Everything above points to one outcome: you leave this site **prepared, not guessing**, with a realistic sense of how you will perform on test day.

---

## What this is not

I am not selling you an instructor-led **course** or bootcamp. I am not selling **PDF braindumps** or printable cheat sheets. I am not asking you to install GNS3 or Packet Tracer just to get through a lab. Be Certified Today is **independent exam prep** — not sponsored by Cisco, CompTIA, or Pearson — built for practice and education.

## How you will see this on the site

### Landing page placement *(conversion funnel)*

Paid and organic traffic lands on exam home pages with **purchase first** — mission copy stays **below** the checkout fold so it does not compete with `begin_checkout`.

| Element | Where it lives | Why |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Tagline** | `funnel-intro`, directly under the SEO eyebrow and above the H1 (`ccna-home.html`, `ccnp-home.html`, `comptia-sec+-home.html`) | Emotional hook for scrollers who skipped purchase; does not push pricing down |
| **30-sec pitch + pillars** | `landing-mission-pitch` inside the compare section, after free-sample CTA, before disclaimer (`#ccna-mission`, `#encor-mission`, `#secplus-mission`) | Catches readers who finished samples + comparison table and still need trust before returning to `#purchase` |
| **Mission FAQ** | Second FAQ item: “Why did you build Be Certified Today?” on each exam home | Long-tail trust + first-person voice for skeptics who read FAQ before buying |
| **Not on purchase fold** | `#purchase` card unchanged | Keeps ad landing ATF focused on price + checkout |

**Canonical tagline on site:** *Practice like test day. Walk in ready.*

| What I care about | What you get |
|-------------------|--------------|
| Current material | v1.1 / v1.2 alignment; questions added over time |
| Exam-realistic practice | Timed sim, drag-and-drop, browser CLI labs, scorecard |
| Trustworthy answers | Explanations tied to official sources |
| Low friction | Free samples; portal access by period; no membership |
| Focus | Study modes without ad clutter in the portal |
| Exam-style UX | Lab tasks and PBQ-style prompts that mirror test day |

**Typical path:** try free samples → unlock full portal access → checkout when you are ready.
