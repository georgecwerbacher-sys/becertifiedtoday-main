A network engineer configures a 4-port EtherChannel between two switches. The EtherChannel comes up, but all traffic flows through only one of the four physical links. What is the most likely cause?

- A. STP is blocking three of the four links
- B. The load-balancing algorithm is hashing all traffic to the same link because the source and destination MAC/IP addresses are the same for all flows

**Stated answer (external):** B
